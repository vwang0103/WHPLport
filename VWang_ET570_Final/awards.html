The Engineering Technology student can apply for one of several awards offered by the department each year. These awards are presented to the recipients in at an award ceremony in the spring semester. Outstanding Engineering Technology Department graduates and students who have completed a minimum of 22 credits in the spring may apply.

Additional details including the, application deadline and application forms are available in the Engineering technology Department office in room T-20. Department awards are an excellent addition to your resume.
